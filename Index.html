Why this sequence works

Email 1 — No Discount

Offering a discount immediately trains customers to abandon carts on purpose. Email 1 recovers easy wins — people who just got distracted — without giving margin away.

Email 2 — Small Discount

By day 2, the remaining shoppers are typically comparing options or seeking reassurance. A modest incentive paired with social proof addresses the two biggest objections: "Is it worth it?" and "Does it actually work?"

Tone — Warm, Not Pushy

Health & wellness buyers are skeptical. Hard-sell tactics destroy trust in this niche. A calm, benefit-led voice converts better and builds longer-term customer relationships.

Cart Reminder — Visual

Showing the product they left behind re-anchors the desire. It reminds them what they wanted, not just that they forgot to check out.
